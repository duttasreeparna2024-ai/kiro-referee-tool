This project compares AWS EC2 and Lambda.  
It helps users choose the right service based on workload, scalability, budget, and maintenance.  
Kiro helps make decisions simple.  
Lambda is best for short, event-driven tasks.  
EC2 is for long-running tasks needing more control.  
Users can see trade-offs and pick wisely.  
Kiro makes understanding easier and faster.
